## Company Overview
**ROZZA USA LLC** is a consulting company registered and active in the State of Florida, United States.

We specialize in structured financial analysis and strategic consulting for individuals and small businesses, with a focus on clarity, risk awareness, and informed decision-making.

---

## Our Approach
Our consulting methodology is based on the analysis of three core variables:

- **Income structure**
- **Expense behavior**
- **Financial objectives**

Using these inputs, we prepare a structured financial assessment that identifies strengths, vulnerabilities, and areas for improvement.  
All findings are delivered directly to the client for independent evaluation and decision-making.

ROZZA USA LLC does **not** manage, hold, or move client funds.
